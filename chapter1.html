Coordinated Strategies

Coordinated Strategies is about the fact that there must be an annual agenda to know when I have to make more efforts in PPC, SEO or social media.

Pay Per Click (PPC)

This is when we pay Ads in Google Adwords or Facebook Ads in order to get viewers to our website.

Before to do that and get thousands of people coming to our website and loose them and throw money to the bin, we need to have the website decently ready to convert this visitors.

Strategy to get ready the website before spend money in Ads.

  • Make tiny changes to the website. Like change colors, text, sizes, or even something bigger.

  • Do A/B testing. This means, generate different versions of your website with tiny modifications.

  • Mini PPC in each of the modified versions of the website.

  • And see which one performs better. This means, get more conversions.

  • Proper PPC campagne to the version of the website that performs best.
